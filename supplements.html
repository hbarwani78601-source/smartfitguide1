Understand what supplements actually help, who should use them, and which ones to avoid.

verified Researched by SmartFitGuide • Beginner-safe recommendations

Who Should Consider Supplements?

  • Beginners struggling to meet protein needs
  • People with busy schedules
  • Those training consistently (3–4x/week)
  • NOT recommended for minors or people with medical conditions without doctor approval

Safety Tips Before You Buy

  • Start with food first: Supplements are meant to supplement, not replace.
  • Read ingredient labels: Know what you are putting in your body.
  • Avoid proprietary blends: Choose products that list exact amounts.
  • Stay hydrated: Especially when using creatine or protein.
  • Follow recommended dosage: More is not always better.
  • Stop immediately if you experience any adverse effects.
