Best Resistance Bands: The Ultimate Space Saver

Don't have room for a full weight rack? Resistance bands offer a full-body workout that fits in a drawer. They are perfect for building muscle, mobility work, and stretching.

Types of Bands

Bands come in different shapes for different goals. Here are the main types:

  • Tube Bands with Handles: The best alternative to dumbbells. Great for pressing and pulling.
  • Mini Loop Bands: Ideal for legs, glutes, and warm-ups. A staple for "booty workouts".
  • Fabric Bands: A more comfortable, non-slip version of loop bands. Won't roll up on your legs.

Who Should Buy Resistance Bands?

Bands are versatile and safe. They are especially good for:

  • Travelers: The only gym you can pack in a carry-on.
  • Joint-Friendly Training: Variable resistance is easier on joints than heavy iron.
  • Glute Activation: Essential for warming up hips and glutes before squats.
