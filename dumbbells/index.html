Detailed Comparison Guide

Feature Why It Matters The Expert Take
Mechanism (Adjustable) Speed of changing weights. Dial systems (like Bowflex) are fast. Spin-locks take forever and kill workout flow.
Shape (Hex vs Round) Safety and convenience. Always get Hex (Hexagonal). Round weights roll away and can crush toes.
Material Coating Floor protection & noise. Rubber or Urethane is essential if you don't have gym flooring. Iron chips and is loud.
Knurling (Grip) How well you can hold it. Look for "Medium" knurling. Too smooth = slip. Too aggressive = torn hands.
