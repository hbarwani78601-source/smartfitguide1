About SmartFitGuide

A research notebook for the modern home athlete. We explore the physiology, physics, and practicalities of training at home.

Our Philosophy

Most fitness sites want to sell you something immediately. We want you to read something first.

Our goal is to act as a filter for the noise. We read the studies, test the equipment, and synthesize the findings into clear, legible notes.

How We Work (Transparency)

Note on Methodology: This site uses AI tools to assist in summarizing research papers and structuring content. However, all claims are verified against public studies. We treat AI as a research assistant, not an author.

Affiliate Disclosure

We are reader-supported. That means when you buy a product via our links (like Amazon), we may earn a small commission. This comes at zero extra cost to you and helps keep this research hub ad-free.

Medical Disclaimer

Content on this site is for educational and informational purposes only. It is exploratory in nature and should not be taken as medical advice.
